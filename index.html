About

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs, specializing in hybrid energy storage systems, microgrids, renewable energy integration, and sustainable zero-emission technologies. With over 9 years of research experience, I focus on developing optimization frameworks for hybrid fuel cell–battery systems. My work aims to enhance the reliability and efficiency of distributed energy resources through advanced techno-economic analysis and experimental lab research.
